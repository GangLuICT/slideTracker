Privacy Policy & Terms of Use

Your privacy is very important to us. So is our server disk space, so it is in our best interest to make sure none of your information is permanently saved. During the use of slideTracker a copy of your presentation will be temporarily stored on our servers to facilitate distribution to your audience. To avoid the need for user registration we will user your MAC address as a temporary identifier for your presentation. All that information will be removed from our servers as soon as you close PowerPoint. In case a connection error occurs we will make sure to delete idle presentations regularily. If you have any questions or concerns please contact us.
